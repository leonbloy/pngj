    IBytesConsumer

    public class ChunkSeqReaderPng
extends ChunkSeqReader
    Adds to ChunkSeqReader the storing of PngChunk, with a PngFactory, and imageInfo + deinterlacer.

    Most usual PNG reading should use this class, or a PngReader, which is a thin wrapper over this.
